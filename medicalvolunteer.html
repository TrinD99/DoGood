Medical Volunteering

  • Medical Mission

    Mission: MedicalMissions.org connects healthcare volunteers with the mission-centered organizations that need them, increasing access to quality healthcare around the world. Registration, search and mission posting is free for all volunteers and organizations.

  • Mercy Care

    Mission: We give people hope where before there was no hope. Maybe that’s the best medicine of all.

  • Children's Healthcare

    Mission: At Children’s, volunteers assist with everything from patient care to customer service and administrative support. Whether you’re helping at the gift shop, making a delivery to a patient’s room, or interacting at the bedside, you will positively affect a family’s hospital experience.

  • Lions Heart

    Mission: Lion’s Heart inspires teens to have a positive impact in their communities through leadership and participation in volunteering, providing needed resources for causes that ignite their passions.

  • Emory Volunteer Services

    Mission: It is the mission of the Emory Healthcare volunteer services department to develop and maintain, dedicated, caring and competent volunteers who embrace the Emory Healthcare Mission; which is to become the leading healthcare provider at the highest level of quality patient care and service.
